GLOBAL CAPITAL MARKET STRATEGY

Emerging Markets are flooded with business opportunities, however the missing link for most emerging markets is their ability to attract and retain global capital. Promoters of projects, leadership of businesses and even public officials with responsibilities for building new infrastructure, are all in the race to secure global capital to expand the horizons of their business and respond to commercial opportunities in their marketplace.

The Platforms and procedure we have adopted include the following:

Global Capital Road Show:

takes corporate leaders on a journey into the World of International Finance, linking and hosting executives who run companies or projects that require global capital, with investment managers, fund managers and investment advisers that are seeking opportunities for investment in emerging markets. Our Road Show experience provides executives promoting capital intensive projects with the dynamics, the understanding, the structure and the partnerships that you will require to fund projects as diverse as re-entry works for a Nigerian Oil field, processing machinery for a cocoa farm in Ghana, Coastal shipping and vessel services for the West African energy industry or consolidation of a South African Entertainment conglomerate. We are particularly great at assisting corporations listed on a local African stock exchange to attract global capital by the issuing of US based depository receipts or global depository receipts.

STRATEGY. PROJECTS. INVESTMENTS.

Strategy

Our experts will define for you what it takes to win the hearts and minds of global investors. We will expose you, on the Road Show, to the fundamentals of securing and winning Global Capital on a sustainable basis. We will support you to secure global capital for your qualified registered projects, and we will offer you unique and exclusive participation in pre-approved projects that may meet your investment profile and tolerance. In some cases, the best strategy may be to seek capital from the Global Capital Markets, issuing securities that will be traded internationally and in other cases it may be straight debt from a tier 1 or a tier 2 financial institution. Whichever the case may be, we will support you, to develop the best strategy that works for you.

Projects

You can register your qualified projects to be promoted on each Road Show. This will mean that our experts will ensure that your projects are matched with potential strategic qualified investors. We support you to repackage and reposition your registered projects to meet the standards and the sophistication of Global Investors’ presentations. Our road show is only the beginning; it is designed to expand your horizon of available capital for your company or your project to include Global Capital. After the Road Show, we will work closely with you to meet the terms of engagement as stipulated by International investors.

Investments

Your goal is to get global capital flowing towards your project and your company, and that’s our goal as well. In this respect, once the Road Show is over, we work with you to meet the requirements set forth by global investors and we ensure that your company and your projects eventually get the investment capital you require. In some exceptional cases, if you qualify, we may include you or your company in a pre-approved global investment project as an early adopter. Early adopters for pre-approved global investment projects are best positioned to receive favorable investment treatment and terms for their own projects from the same investment funds that have funded the pre-approved global investment project.

LOCAL CONTENT STRATEGIC INITIATIVE

About Local Content Strategic Initiative

The Local Content Strategic Initiative is designed to support local economies of communities, regions, states and nations that support the operations of large scale extractive and heavy industries. The LCSI does this by providing a path to the development and inclusion of local entrepreneurs, communities, youths and women. We build continuous and sustainable support structures for Local Content certified companies operating in the oil, gas and energy industry value chain. The Local Content Strategic Initiative has a number of schemes, programs and projects designed to impact the local, regional and national economies by creating new entrepreneurs, promoting the creation of new jobs and providing a framework for the empowerment and support of communities, youths and women. It is about enhancing the capacity of the Nation to be in a positon to support the large economy and the industry with the right skills, the right international partnerships, the right technology and the right equipment and the right financing. The Local Content Strategic Initiative supports the mentorship of Small and Medium businesses in the value chain of the energy industry so that local entrepreneurs can build capacity to become global players within short time frames.
