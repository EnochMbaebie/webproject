The Nigeria Leadership Summit

The Nigeria Leadership Summit is Nigeria’s largest and most attended privately organized summit on Leadership in Nigeria. It is an annual event that brings together leaders from all works of life to discuss the challenges of Leadership in Nigeria and Africa. The Summit provides a platform for the generation of ideas and solutions which are shared with the entire Country and with its Leadership. The Summit started in 2012 and has been hosted every year since then. It records over four thousand delegates annually, who attend in person and another fifty thousand who participate online.
